# 🧊 Glacial Zombie Speed Reference

## Speed Hierarchy (pixels per second)

### 🧟 **Basic Zombie** - 0.1 px/s
- **Baseline glacial pace**
- Takes 80 seconds to cross 1 cell
- Takes ~12 minutes to cross entire lawn

### 🧟‍♂️ **Conehead Zombie** - 0.08 px/s  
- **20% slower than basic**
- Cone weight slows them down
- Takes 100 seconds to cross 1 cell

### 🧟‍♀️ **Buckethead Zombie** - 0.05 px/s
- **50% slower than basic**
- Heavy bucket makes them crawl
- Takes 160 seconds (2.7 min) to cross 1 cell

### 🏃‍♂️ **Runner Zombie** - 0.2 px/s
- **2x basic speed (still glacial!)**
- "Fast" zombie that's still super slow
- Takes 40 seconds to cross 1 cell

### 🦘 **Pole Vaulter** - 0.15 px/s
- **1.5x basic speed**
- Slightly more agile
- Takes 53 seconds to cross 1 cell

### 🎈 **Balloon Zombie** - 0.12 px/s
- **1.2x basic speed**
- Floats slowly through air
- Takes 67 seconds to cross 1 cell

### 👑 **Boss Zombie** - 0.06 px/s
- **40% slower than basic**
- Massive size slows movement
- Takes 133 seconds (2.2 min) to cross 1 cell

## Speed Comparison
- **Fastest**: Runner (0.2 px/s) - still takes 6 minutes to cross lawn
- **Slowest**: Boss (0.06 px/s) - takes 20 minutes to cross lawn
- **Most Common**: Basic (0.1 px/s) - takes 12 minutes to cross lawn

*All speeds maintain the glacial, contemplative gameplay experience!* 🧊🧟‍♂️
